Frequently Asked Questions

How do I use the weather-based recipe recommendations?

Allow location access when prompted, and Recipe Website will automatically suggest recipes based on current weather conditions. Cold weather? Try soups and stews. Hot day? Perfect for fresh salads and light dishes.

Can I save my favorite recipes?

Currently, we're working on a bookmark feature. For now, you can bookmark pages in your browser or take screenshots of your favorite recipes!

How accurate are the ingredient substitutions?

Our smart substitution system is based on culinary science and regional availability. We recommend trying suggested alternatives, but always consider your taste preferences and dietary restrictions.

Why do you need my location?

Your location helps us provide weather-based recommendations, suggest local ingredients, and offer appropriate measurement units. You can disable location access at any time in your browser settings.

Can I contribute my own recipes?

We love community contributions! Please contact us at hello@plateup.com with your recipe ideas. We're particularly interested in authentic regional dishes and family favorites.
